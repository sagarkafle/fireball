Black Cardamom

The aromatic organic cardamom has a pungent aroma with a citrus and eucalyptus flavor. Black cardamom is dried over an open fire and smoke to provide distinct smoky aroma and flavor.

Black cardamom we supply are grown in easter hilly region of Nepal. This is rich in anti-bacterial properties and Vitamin-C , which is a good antioxidant. These are being used as spice in south Asian delicacies over centuries especially to give savory flavor. This black gold’s harvesting season is during October and November.

About Us

Fire Ball Trading Company Pvt. Ltd. was initially known as Gati Enterprise and founded in 2014 as a trading and manufacturing company. Business model has been restructured and company registration has been renewed in 2020 as a complete trading company.

 

Our core export products are Black Cardamom, Green Tea, Cinnamon and Himalayan Dog Chews. These products are being supplied to India, United States of America and Philippines for more than 5 years. Amongst these products, dog chews (Hard cheese dog treat made of cow and yak milk in Himalayas) is the most popular in American market as a premium dog treat. We tailor product packing, private labeling and shipping to cater the customer’s requirement.

 

We have been importing and distributing premium quality breakfast cereals, edible oils (RBD corn and Virgin Olive) and Chestnut (seasonal) since 2015 from Portugal, Italy, India & China. All our products are being distributed in five major cities of Nepal (Kathmandu, Pokhara, Butwal, Chitwan, Dharan).

 

The keystone of our business is our profound knowledge on dog chews, premium FMCG and have them tailored according to customer’s requirement.

 

Fire Ball Trading Company is committed to provide seamless quality product/service by keeping our customers in the heart of everything we do.
